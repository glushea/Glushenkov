Alex Glushenkov - Cities I LOVE

Jerusalem

Its the best city in the world. This city, a timeless tapestry of cultures, religions, and history, weaves together the threads of humanity's shared heritage, captivating hearts with its sacred magnificence.

Moscow

A vibrant metropolis where history dances with modernity, beckons visitors with its iconic landmarks, rich cultural tapestry, and the unmistakable pulse of a city that never sleeps.

Minsk

A hidden gem nestled in the heart of Belarus, exudes an enchanting allure with its picturesque streets, elegant architecture, and a resilient spirit that whispers tales of a captivating past and a promising future.

Paris

The epitome of timeless elegance and artistic inspiration, unveils its enchanting beauty through the grandeur of the Eiffel Tower, the romantic embrace of the Seine River, and the artistic masterpieces that adorn its boulevards, forever captivating the souls of wanderers and dreamers alike.

Buenos Aires

A vibrant mosaic of passion and tango, where the rhythm of life intertwines with the rich flavors of Argentine cuisine, captivating visitors with its lively energy, majestic architecture, and a profound appreciation for art, literature, and the art of living.

London

A kaleidoscope of tradition and innovation, proudly wears its crown of iconic landmarks, from the regal Buckingham Palace to the towering Big Ben, while its bustling streets echo with the footsteps of history, bridging the gap between the past and the pulsating heartbeat of a global metropolis.

Tel Aviv - Jaffa

A captivating fusion of old and new, where ancient history whispers through the ancient port of Jaffa and modern innovation ignites the vibrant streets of Tel Aviv, creating a dynamic cityscape that embraces the Mediterranean breeze, radiates cultural diversity, and invites visitors to immerse themselves in a coastal haven of art, cuisine, and unforgettable experiences.

New York

The city that never sleeps, pulsates with an electrifying energy as towering skyscrapers reach for the sky, yellow taxis race through bustling streets, and a cultural mosaic of diversity weaves together the vibrant tapestry of a place where dreams are born and possibilities know no bounds.
